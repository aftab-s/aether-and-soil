The AETHER & SOIL Storefront Prompt
System Role: Act as a world-class Lead Frontend Engineer and Luxury Brand Designer.

Objective: Create a high-end, single-page React landing page for a specialty coffee roastery named AETHER & SOIL. The brand philosophy is "Ritual meets Origin"—blending the refined art of the pour-over with the rugged, volcanic landscapes where the beans are grown.

Visual Aesthetic & Vibe
Color Palette: Use the "Mocca Mousse" trend. Base of deep espresso and velvet chocolate. Accents of "Oat Milk" Cream, Burnt Terracotta (to represent soil), and a muted Forest Moss.

Typography: A high-contrast, elegant Serif (like Cormorant Garamond) for headings to evoke the "Ritual." A technical, monospace font (like JetBrains Mono) for tasting notes and altitude data to evoke the "Origin."

Layout: A Bento Grid for the "Process" section to highlight the journey from farm to cup.

Page Sections (Single Page)
Immersive Hero: A full-screen "Scrollytelling" section. As the user scrolls, a high-res 3D render of a minimalist coffee bag rotates, while coffee grounds transition into a steaming cup.

Heading: "AETHER & SOIL: The Ritual of the Morning. The Soul of the Earth."

The Extraction Anatomy (Bento Grid):

Tile 1 (Aether): A macro video loop of a slow pour-over "bloom."

Tile 2 (Soil): High-detail texture shot of volcanic soil and raw green coffee cherries.

Tile 3 (The Specs): A technical breakdown using the Monospace font: Altitude: 1,900m | Process: Natural | Notes: Jasmine, Stone Fruit.

The Daily Ritual Lookbook: A multidirectional carousel showing the coffee in two worlds: A sleek, minimalist home office setup and a rugged campfire brew in the mountains.

The Subscription Access: A minimalist "Join the Harvest" form for the July 2026 launch. Use a satisfying liquid-fill micro-interaction on the "Secure My Bag" button.

Technical Footer: Centered navigation with "Origin Maps" and "Brew Guides" links.

Technical Requirements
Mobile-First: Navigation and "Shop Now" CTAs positioned for easy thumb access.

Micro-interactions: Parallax scrolling on "Steam" overlays and a "breathing" pulse effect on the CTA buttons.

Performance: Next-gen image formats (WebP/AVIF) and lazy-loading to maintain a "green" performance score.

No Backend: Front-end prototype using local state for the subscription form.
